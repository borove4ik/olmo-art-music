Bio

Olmo (Olmo van der Mast, 1995) is a singer-songwriter from Delft (The Netherlands). His songs range from personal stories to protest anthems. Influenced by artists like Glen Hansard, Jonathan Jeremiah, and Tom Waits, Olmo's music blends elements of pop, rock, and blues. In 2014, Olmo emerged as a promising talent by winning the first edition of Young Quite Quiet, a contest for young singer-songwriters. Following his 2014 EP, Through This Time, Break Away marks his first full-length album, showcasing a mix of intimate acoustic pieces and vibrant blues rock.
